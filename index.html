You will be given a random number of orders at the start of the game. There are four meal components below. By clicking on each item, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each meal item is hidden from your until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts over, the game will change the values of each meal item. Select any meal item with your mouse to start playing.
