You will be given the number of Orders Needed at the start of the game. There are four meal components below. By clicking on each item, you will add a specific amount of points to your Orders Fulfilled.

You win the game by matching your Orders Fulfilled to Orders Needed. You lose the game if your Orders Fulfilled goes above the Orders Needed.

The value of each meal item is hidden from your until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts over, the game will change the values of each meal item. Select any meal item with your mouse to start playing.
